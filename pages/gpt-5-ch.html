What’s new with GPT‑5?

Major upgrade across the board

OpenAI introduces GPT‑5 as a smarter, faster, more accurate model that significantly outperforms GPT‑4 and previous versions in coding, writing, reasoning, and even health-related inquiries.

A unified model with multi-tiered intelligence

Instead of choosing between models, GPT‑5 automatically routes your prompt to the most appropriate variant. For simpler queries, a lightweight version (like “mini” or “nano”) handles it; for tougher, more complex tasks, it engages a deeper reasoning variant known as “GPT‑5 thinking” .

Smarter pricing and tiers

There are multiple variants to suit different needs and budgets—from GPT‑5 (standard), to GPT‑5 mini (cost-efficient), and GPT‑5 nano (ultra-fast and budget-friendly). Free users get access with lower usage caps; Plus and Pro users enjoy higher limits and even more capabilities.

Multimodality & agentic abilities

GPT‑5 handles text, images, potentially audio — plus it's excellent at chaining together actions (tool calls), planning, and coding. One demo had it build a full website with frontend UI in minutes—planning, scaffolding, coding, building, summarizing, and suggesting next steps autonomously.

Enhanced safety & real-world performance

OpenAI claims GPT‑5 lowers hallucination rates by a significant margin and improves reliability, especially in domains like health and finance. Safeguards are in place to help the model gracefully handle tasks it cannot solve.
